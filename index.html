About Me :)

I’m a passionate designer studying a Bachelor of Design (Interaction) at the University of Sydney. I’m eager to work on projects with the goal of making a positive change. I believe meaningful solutions come from connections with the communities you are designing for. My work does this by leveraging my experience in public speaking and writing in order to connect to users. My graphic design internship at Hazen Agency allows me to turn these user needs and connections into real design solutions.
